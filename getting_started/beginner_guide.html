Choosing Your First 3D Printer

Most beginners start with an FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) printer because it is affordable, easy to use, and widely supported.

  • Look for good community support
  • Auto bed leveling is a big plus
  • Open-source firmware and parts availability help long-term

Understanding Filament

Filament is the material your printer uses to create objects. There are many types, but beginners should start simple.

PLA

The best filament for beginners. Easy to print, low warping, and works on nearly all printers.
