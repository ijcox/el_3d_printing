Filaments & Materials (FDM)

PLA

Easy to print, beginner‑friendly, biodegradable filament.

PETG

Durable, flexible, and more heat‑resistant than PLA.

Strong and heat‑resistant, but requires controlled conditions.

Nylon

Extremely strong and flexible engineering filament.

TPU

Flexible filament for rubber‑like parts.

Reinforced filaments for strength and rigidity.
